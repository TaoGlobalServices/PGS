Cold Storage & Logistics

Farm-to-Port Integrity

We understand that in the fresh produce business, temperature control is everything. Oasis Plus manages the entire logistics chain to ensure zero break in the cold cycle.

  • Pre-Cooling Facilities: Rapid temperature reduction post-harvest to lock in freshness.
  • Custom Retail Packaging: Branded corrugated boxes, plastic crates, and vacuum seals.
  • Air & Sea Forwarding: Direct coordination with airlines for high-perishables and sea liners for bulk coconut shipments.
  • Phytosanitary Clearance: We handle all necessary export documentation and customs paperwork.
